Presentation

Presentation is important to food when you are trying to make the product look appetizing or photogenic. Restaurants usually try to make
their dishes have a good presentation on their menus in order to attract customers to buy that certain dish or dishes. Not only do restaurants
use presentation to their advantage, but so do bakeries. While there may be different intentions between the two, each of them rely on this aspect
to attract their customers and make them buy it.

Restaurants

As said before, restaurants try to perfect their presentation in order to persuade customers. However, they also try to consistently make their
meals have a good presentation in order to get good ratings on yelp and have a good reputation.Some chefs in restaurants take pride in their cooking and
only expect the best from themselves, which includes the quality of the meal, theq quality of the ingredients used, and the presentation itself.


Bakeries

Just like Restaurants, Bakeries want to have a good prepartation in order to sell the item. While this may be true, most bakers take the time
to put a lot of effort and havea good presentation for pride. Bakeries aren't as rushed as Restaurants, so they are able to take the necessary
time to put in a lot of effort to make their cakes or pastries look nice and presentable.

One bakery that runs of family and pride is Carlo's Bake Shop. Carlo's Bake Shop is commonly
known for its famous cakes with lots of detail and taste in general. Carlo's Bake Shop has been going on since 1910, making it over 100 years old! The bakery
also has a TV show on TLC called "Cake Boss". This televised program is about how Buddy (the owner) and his team are put up with impossible requests for parties
and create a cake so spectacular that it makes you not want to eat it because of its beauty.
