Meals of the Day

Breakfast

Breakfast is the first meal of the day, giving you the energy you need to either go to work or head to school.
This is one of the most important meals due to how essential it is to stay awake and energized.
Breakfast should normally be consumed around 7:00 am because it is the best time to get the required energy and
make it to Lunch time.

While eating breakfast is essential, some people struggle to make time for this meal because they either wake up too late
or they running late for work or school. Some quick and easy breakfast meals include:

  1. Cereal
  2. Oatmeal/Porridge
  3. Yogurt
  4. Granola Bar
  5. Toast

Lunch

The second meal of the day is Lunch. Lunch is commonly confused with people whether it is important or not.
Well, Lunch is important, just like Breakfast and Dinner, because of energy. Running on low energy for any device or human
outputs low quality products. Lunch should be consumed at about 12:45 or noon. This is because the energy that was obtained
at breakfast should be running out and more is required. Eating lunch will help whomever get through the day until Dinner comes around.

Some optional lunches that could appeal to anyone include:

  1. Sandwich
  2. Salad
  3. Wrap
  4. Soup
  5. Leftover Dinner from last night

Dinner

The final meal of the day is Dinner. Like Breakfast and Lunch, Dinner is important, however Dinner is actually more essential than
The two other meals. This is because you need to make sure you have enough energy to last you the whole night and make up for the energy
you lost since Lunch. A good time to eat dinner is around 6:30 pm or just before 7:00 pm.

Dinner is also a good time to catch up with relatives or just family in general. You can discuss about recent news or anything that
happened throughout the day.
